Learn about FDRI

The Floods and Droughts Research Infrastructure (FDRI) is a UK research infrastructure that brings together high-resolution, whole-system hydrological monitoring with open data and analysis tools.

This Digital Platform is the front door to FDRI data: a searchable catalogue, interactive explorers for time series and spatial data, notebooks for gridded and LiDAR products, and the open code behind it all. It is in active development — use Share feedback to help shape it.
