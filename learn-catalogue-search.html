Learn about the Catalogue Search

The Catalogue Search helps you find exactly the data you need from across the FDRI holdings. Search by keyword, then narrow your results with filters for catalogue, type, topic, licence and record type.

Switch to the Spatial tab to filter by area — enter latitude and longitude bounds, or draw a box on the map — and combine spatial and metadata filters to focus on a catchment or region.

Every result links through to a full metadata record, so you can understand a dataset before you use it.

An annotated walkthrough for this tool is on the way. In the meantime, open the tool to explore, or try a Data Story.
