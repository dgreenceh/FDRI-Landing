Sub-daily meteorology and river flow observations from a long-term upland catchment experiment in Plynlimon, Wales, 1968–2010

This dataset contains hourly automatic weather station (AWS) and 15-minute river flow gauging station data from sites in the Plynlimon (Pumlumon) area of upland mid-Wales, representing the headwaters of the Rivers Severn (Hafren) and Wye (Gwy). A range of instrumentation across 6 AWS sites and 10 gauging stations delivered meteorological and river flow data from 1968 to 2010 to explore changing hydrometeorology as part of the long-term Plynlimon catchment monitoring study.
