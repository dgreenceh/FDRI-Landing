FAQs

What data is on the platform?

UK-wide hydrological datasets across several types — time series, geospatial, gridded and LiDAR — from FDRI monitoring sites and partner networks.

Do I need an account?

Browsing the catalogue and explorers is open. Some downloads or notebooks may ask you to sign in; this will be confirmed as the platform develops.

How do I cite a dataset?

Each catalogue record carries the citation and licence details for that dataset. Open the record from any search result.

Where's the code?

The tools, notebooks and services are open source — see GitHub Repos.

How do I report a problem?

Use Share feedback at the top of any page. We read everything.
